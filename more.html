Report an accessibility problem

We want you to be able to access all your favourite locations on your mobility scooter. We actively work with local communities and relevant authorities to support accessibility in your local community. We can all work together to support accessibility throughout North Queensland.

If you come across a problem accessing a location on your mobility scooter you can report the problem to your local council through Snap Send Solve.

Supporting Local Community Connections

One of our goals is to help improve the lives of residents with limited mobility through the introduction of scooters and initiation of programs that foster community connection and keep people socially connected.

We’re proud to have partnered with occupational therapists, physiotherapists and GPs throughout Townsville and the North Queensland region to spread the word about all the benefits of electric mobility in Queensland.
