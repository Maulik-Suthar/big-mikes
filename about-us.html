About us

A BIT ABOUT US

We aim to give our customers a real complete customer package, from the very first time they walk in the door, right through to the sales, servicing, and repairs. Customers can come for a chat about their new mobility and disability aid and enjoy one of our barista-made coffees or tea from our in-house Joylicious Cafe designed just for our customers. We have a great range of new and second-hand products available everything from our custom designed Mobility Scooters, our KS-1 top of the line wheelchair, Showerbuddy, beds, hoist, or any other Daily Living Aid. If we don’t have it in stock just ask and we will source it for you at a competitive price and in a very timely fashion.

  • Have a test drive or even try before you buy
  • Come and enjoy our now famous Joylicious Cafe coffee whislt you are browsing around and obtaining real customer advise and service.
All our scooters sold (both new and second-hand) come with a free 3-month service to ensure that is performing as it should.
  • We offer a mobile service for all scooters
  • We offer free pick up and return on all repairs

Our customer service goes well beyond selling your mobility or disability products. When you purchase anything from us, we’re here to support you and your family with any of your servicing, repairs, or modification needs. Should you wish to bring your item in for a service, repair, or modification we offer a comfortable place to sit, read a magazine, enjoy a free coffee or tea at our new in-store Joylicious Cafe designed for you our customer.

We at a truly local and family-owned and operated business with over 60 years of engineering expertise and are fully qualified to offer services on mobility scooters, wheelchairs, and all disability items. Big Mike’s Mobility aims to provide a real one-stop-shop and will assist in the Queensland registration of the scooters if required, insurance needs for your new scooter, and offer many finance options customised to your needs to make your scooter affordable. We aim to assist our customers in all your mobility needs and requirements and strive to make available the best service and the right price for all people regardless of your social, financial, or upbringing in life. Our goal is to bring back the freedom of mobility and independence that is lost with time and/or disabilities by making our products and service affordable and dependable for all our customers.

WHY BUY FROM US

Our claim of having the lowest price and best quality products is guaranteed. In addition to this, our free home demonstrations and free ongoing training whenever you need it sets us apart from the competitors. Servicing your items should be as easy as making a phone call. Our mobile workshop also enables us to visit you to complete any maintenance and repairs anywhere in Townsville and the North Queensland region. We also offer free loan products (when available) to all our customers.

Because we’re a local family-owned company, based in Townsville and Rockhampton regions, we support many local community-based services and sporting groups including Red Cross , BlueCare, Baptist Care, and Bolton Clarke. It’s all about improving the quality of life for everyone in our local community. Because of our cost-efficient family company structure, we can pass on large discounts direct from our manufacturer to you and provide a high-quality product that will give years of enjoyment.
