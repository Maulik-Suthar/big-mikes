Description

The BT406 delivers amazing standard features like LED lighting for maximum brightness, performance, and minimum maintenance. A bottle holder, two rearview mirrors, 8.0A heavy-duty charger, and two USB charging ports for small electronic devices are also included as standard. There’s more comfort with the new tiller providing infinite tilt steering adjustment for ergonomic user positioning. Get the latest driving assistance technology with the easy to read control panel with integrated digital display.
