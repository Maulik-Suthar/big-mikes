Servicing Your
Mobility Scooter

The Big Mike’s Mobility Servicing Department specializes in the repair, service, modification, and preventive maintenance of all powered wheelchairs, mobility scooters, lifting hoists both floor and ceiling, chairs and beds, and many more mobility aid items, throughout the North Queensland region. We are not only committed to conducting service repairs or modifications on the brands we sell, but our service scope also covers all brands, models, and types of mobility equipment. As a result of this, Big Mike’s Mobility has invested heavily in training and development of our staff to ensure we provide our clients with the best of service each time ensuring your device is operating at peak performance and maintained throughout its working life. Our comprehensive service plan is second to none and seen and a leading service scoop amongst our competitors. Our experienced technicians are ready to tackle any repairs or modifications to your Mobility and Disability Aid products, to keep you mobile and comfortable.

We have prioritized access to spare parts and equipment which are available to purchase online, over the phone, or over the counter! Need servicing? call us today on 07 4427 5622 and we will get you up and running!

There is also no need to come to us, we’ll come to you. If you live anywhere in the North Queensland region, just call us and we’ll schedule a time that’s convenient for you to receive your mobility or disability item servicing. We’ll bring our own mobile workshop to you or we can arrange to have your item brought to one of our workshops.

We’re ready to do whatever we can to help you regain your independence and freedom. From servicing, repairs, or modifications, you’ll find everything you need to enjoy a greater friendly and helpful approach through Big Mike’s Mobility.

Should a repair or service require an extended period of time to complete we’ll give you a free rental item to assist you whilst we are fixing yours.
