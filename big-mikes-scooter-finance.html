Big Mike’s Scooter
Finance

Would you like a new mobility scooter now? Are you concerned about a used mobility scooter costing you more in the long run? We can offer you mobility scooter finance.

We can help find your independence and freedom today by offering zero interest mobility scooter finance with no hidden fees, ever! Since we provide the scooter finance ourselves, there’s no finance company and no credit checks.

To receive your new mobility scooter, you’ll just need to pay a 50% deposit and the remaining 50% will be paid over 12 monthly repayments, with a minimum of $100/month.

For example, if your scooter cost is $2,400 then the deposit is $1,200 and you’ll pay 12 payments of $100/month, that’s it!

If you’d like mobility scooter finance on your new mobility scooter, please contact us today.

Finance is available through My Aged Care and DVA . Talk to your My Aged Care or DVA office for more information about finance options.

Big Mike’s is also a registered provider under the NDIS. So, if you are a participant of NDIS we can help you get the right scooter for your needs at little or no costs to you. Just ask one of the friendly staff at Big Mike’s and we will be happy to assist you with your NDIS claim.

Humm Finance offers interest free repayments with pre-approval for up to $10,000 and repay over 6, 12, 24 months all the way up to 60 months.
