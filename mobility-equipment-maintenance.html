Mobility Equipment
Maintenance

Big Mike’s Preventative Maintenance & Asset Management Programs for mobility equipment are tailored to meet our customer’s requirements. We can provide maintenance services for hospital beds, lifting hoists, alternating pressure mattresses, shower transport commodes, commercial trolleys, pressure/positioning chairs, wheelchairs, shower trolleys, walking frames, weigh scales (not calibration), electrical test & tag (additional), sanitisers and macerators.

Services include:

Assessment and Audit

  • Asset number sticker generated
  • Detailed description of equipment, entered into Service Call Management software package.
  • Condition / Hazard assessment report generated
  • Equipment maintenance folder provided

Maintenance Program Developed in Conjunction with Client

  • Recommended equipment service periods used as basis for maintenance cycles
  • Pre-plan / notified in advance of maintenance period
  • Assets bundled into “sensible” groups per location
  • Pre-Approval for conducting repairs outside of maintenance contract scope

Experienced Service Technicians

  • Industry trained ; Test & Tag certified; Police Checked

Report Upon Completion

  • Equipment condition summarised
  • Maintenance carried out summarised
  • Quote on additional major repairs required
  • Report of history of expenditure per equipment

  • Service what we sell and back it up with full factory warranties.
